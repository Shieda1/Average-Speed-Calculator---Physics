What is the average speed?
Velocity is the directional speed of an object in motion as an indication of it's rate of change in position as observed from a particular frame of reference and as measured by a particular standard of time.
How to calculate average speed?
To calculate average speed you should use the following formula:
v' = Δx / Δt
where:
v' = average velocity
Δx = displacement
Δt = change in time
Why use average speed?
The average speed is used in order to describe the rate of motion over a path.
